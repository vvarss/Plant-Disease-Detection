Welcome to the Tomato Disease Identifier

This website is built to assist farmers and agricultural workers in protecting their tomato crops.

By simply uploading a picture of your tomato plant leaves, the system will analyze the image using intelligent classification algorithms to determine if the leaf is:

  • Healthy
  • Infected with Early Blight
  • Infected with Late Blight

Our goal is to help farmers make quick and informed decisions to protect their crops, improve yield, and reduce losses caused by plant diseases.
